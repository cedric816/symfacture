FEATURES

- user can create an account and access secure and private space
- settings: user can choose numbering system, quote and invoice style (custom logo and colors), and provide contact details
- catalog: addition, modification of a product or service in the catalog
- customer: addition, modification of a customer in the customer file
- quote: creation and sending by email to the customer, picking product/service in the catalog
- invoice: creation from scratch or from an existing quote
- quote and invoice are visible in PDF

FRENCH TAX STANDARDS RESPECT

- quote and invoice: numbering followed and not modifiable
- quote and invoice informations not modifiable after validation
